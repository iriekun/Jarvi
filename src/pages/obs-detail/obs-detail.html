What is Ice Condition Observation?

Snow and ice observations have been using for climate change research, the preparation of flood forecasting, assessment of roofs from snow load and the security situation awareness. Data reported will be used for research purposes and thus complement the observations of the authorities.

There are 3 main categories of ice condition on lake.

No ice
Water has not yet frozen or has already completely melted. Partially cover
Water is a partially frozen or melted, but the whole aspect of the circuit is not frozen. Compact ice cover
Water is in the form of ice, and ice thickness can be measured.
